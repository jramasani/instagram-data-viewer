Privacy Policy

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything happens locally in your browser.

Our Commitment to Privacy

We take your privacy extremely seriously. This tool was built with a "privacy-first" approach, ensuring that your Instagram data remains completely under your control at all times.

How Your Data is Handled

  • Local Processing: All data processing happens entirely in your browser. Your Instagram data never leaves your device.
  • No Server Upload: We don't have any servers to store your data - everything is processed client-side using JavaScript.
  • No Tracking: We don't use any analytics tools, cookies, or tracking mechanisms.
  • No Data Retention: Once you close or refresh the page, all processed data is automatically cleared from your browser's memory.

Technical Details

This tool uses modern browser technologies to process your Instagram data files:

  • The JSZip library is used to extract your ZIP file locally in your browser
  • All data parsing and analysis happens in your browser's JavaScript engine
  • No external APIs are called (except for loading the JSZip library and Buy Me a Coffee button)
  • The source code is open and available for inspection

Your Control

You maintain complete control over your data:

  • You can verify that no network requests are made with your data using your browser's developer tools
  • You can use the tool offline after the initial page load
  • You can delete all data by simply closing or refreshing the page

Updates to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally to reflect any necessary changes to our privacy practices. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
